Our Story

More Than a Meal —
It's a Feeling

Be-Nice Restaurant & Bar was born from a simple but powerful belief: everyone deserves great food, served with love.

Located in the heart of Tema Community 5 (behind Temasco), we grew from a beloved catering service into a full restaurant and bar — serving daily food packs, corporate teams, and memorable events across Greater Accra.

Led by Chef Afia, our kitchen uses only fresh, quality ingredients to bring authentic Ghanaian recipes alongside delicious continental options.
